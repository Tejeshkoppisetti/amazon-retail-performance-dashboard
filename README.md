📊 Amazon Retail Performance Dashboard
🔍 Project Overview
A comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing retail sales performance, profitability, and customer segmentation to help business leaders make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Business Objectives
Understand overall sales & profit performance
Identify high-performing product categories
Analyze customer segments contributing most to revenue
Investigate regional profit differences
Visualize monthly trends and shipment costs
🛠 Tools & Techniques Used
Power BI Desktop
DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)
Power Query (Data transformation & cleaning)
Data Modeling
Visual storytelling & dashboard design
📈 Key Metrics
KPI	Value
Total Sales	2.30M
Total Profit	286.40K
Total Quantity Sold	38K
Profit Margin	12.47%
Total Shipment Cost	87.03K
💡 Key Insights from Dashboard
Technology products are the highest contributors to sales.
Consumer segment shows the strongest revenue performance.
West region leads in total profit among all regions.
Some products with high sales may have lower profit contribution → signals pricing or cost issues.
